About

El Mesón de Juan Valle is located in the capital of Guanajuato, and thanks to its location, It is 150 meters from the Main Garden and 300 meters from the Jardín de la Unión. Similarly, It has many of the most famous tourist attractions in the area, such as the Callejón del Beso and the Hidalgo Market. There is free Wi-Fi in all rooms and free parking service. Come and live an unforgettable vacation.

Our rooms are perfect for you. They have individual beds, flat screen TV with PayTV, free WiFi and many other things that will make your stay very pleasent
